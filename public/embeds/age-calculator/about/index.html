About Age Calculator

Age Calculator is a free, fast, and privacy-friendly suite of date and age tools. Our goal is to make it easy for anyone to answer common date-related questions — no sign-up, no ads that slow you down, and no personal data collected.

Our Tools

  • Age Calculator — exact age in years, months, weeks, and days.
  • Birthday Countdown — live timer to your next birthday.
  • Date Difference — duration between any two dates.
  • Day of the Week — what day any date falls on.
  • Zodiac Sign — your sign, birthstone, and birth flower.
  • Retirement Calculator — years until your retirement date.

Accuracy

All calculations run entirely in your browser using JavaScript's built-in Date object, which accounts for leap years, varying month lengths, and time zones. Results are calculated as of the current date and time on your device.
