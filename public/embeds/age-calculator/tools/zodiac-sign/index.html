Frequently Asked Questions

What are the 12 zodiac signs in order?

The 12 zodiac signs in calendar order are: Aries (Mar 21), Taurus (Apr 20), Gemini (May 21), Cancer (Jun 21), Leo (Jul 23), Virgo (Aug 23), Libra (Sep 23), Scorpio (Oct 23), Sagittarius (Nov 22), Capricorn (Dec 22), Aquarius (Jan 20), and Pisces (Feb 19). Each sign spans roughly 30 days of the year.

What are the four elements in astrology?

Astrology divides the 12 signs into four elements. Fire signs (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius) are passionate and energetic. Earth signs (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn) are practical and grounded. Air signs (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius) are intellectual and communicative. Water signs (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces) are emotional and intuitive.

What is a cusp sign?

A cusp sign refers to being born within a few days of when one sign transitions to the next — for example, between Aries and Taurus around April 19–20. Traditional astrology holds that you are definitively one sign or the other depending on the exact date (and sometimes the exact time) of your birth. The concept of cusp personalities, while popular, is not recognized in classical Western astrology.

What is the difference between a sun sign and a moon sign?

Your sun sign is determined by your birth date and represents your core identity, ego, and outward personality. Your moon sign is determined by where the moon was positioned at your exact birth time and location, and represents your emotional inner world. Most horoscopes are based on your sun sign, but many astrologers consider the moon sign equally or more important for understanding personality.

Which zodiac signs are most compatible?

In traditional astrology, signs of the same element tend to be highly compatible — Fire with Fire, Earth with Earth, Air with Air, and Water with Water. Complementary element pairings — Fire with Air, and Earth with Water — are also often considered excellent matches. For example, Aries (Fire) is typically most compatible with Leo, Sagittarius, and Gemini.

How accurate are zodiac sign personality descriptions?

Zodiac personality descriptions are generalizations rooted in thousands of years of astrological tradition and should be treated as a broad framework rather than a precise profile. A full birth chart — accounting for all planetary positions, your rising sign, and the houses — provides far more nuance than a sun sign alone. Many people find the descriptions resonate broadly while not matching perfectly in every detail.

Zodiac Elements Explained

The 12 signs are grouped into four classical elements. Fire signs (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius) tend to be dynamic, enthusiastic, and action-oriented. Earth signs (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn) are practical, reliable, and security-focused. Air signs (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius) are intellectual, social, and idea-driven. Water signs (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces) are emotional, intuitive, and deeply perceptive.

Zodiac Sign Compatibility

Astrological compatibility is traditionally assessed by comparing the elements and modalities (Cardinal, Fixed, Mutable) of two signs. Signs of the same element naturally understand each other, while complementary element pairings — Fire and Air, Earth and Water — often create balanced, harmonious relationships.
