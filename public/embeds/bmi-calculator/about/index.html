About BMI Calculator

Our mission is to make health information free and accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

BMI Calculator provides free, accurate, and easy-to-use health calculators for people around the world. We believe that basic health information should be available to everyone, without sign-ups, paywalls, or complicated interfaces.

Our tools are built on established medical formulas and reviewed for accuracy. We include clear explanations of what each result means — and equally important, what it doesn't mean.
