Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Calculator inputs: Height, weight, age, and other measurements you enter into our calculators are processed entirely in your browser. We do not store, transmit, or have access to these values.

Analytics: We use anonymized analytics to understand aggregate site usage (e.g., page views, device types). This does not include personally identifiable information.

Advertising: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You can opt out via Google Ads Settings.

2. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for analytics and advertising purposes. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect the display of advertisements but will not affect calculator functionality.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense — display advertising (Google Privacy Policy applies)
  • Google Analytics — anonymized usage analytics

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

4. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The BMI for Children calculator does not collect or store the data entered.

5. Data Security

Since all calculator computations happen client-side in your browser, your health measurements never leave your device. We do not have servers that store user-entered health data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@bmicalculator.com
