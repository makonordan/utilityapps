About ImageTools

ImageTools is a free suite of browser-based image utilities. We built it because most image tools either require an account, upload your files to a server, or charge a fee for basic functionality.

Every tool on this site runs entirely in your browser using the Web APIs available in modern browsers. Your images are never sent to any server — they stay on your device at all times.

Our Tools

  • Image Compressor — Reduce file size up to 90% without visible quality loss.
  • Image Resizer — Resize images to exact pixel dimensions or by percentage.
  • Bulk Compressor — Compress dozens of images at once and download as a ZIP.
  • WebP Converter — Convert JPG and PNG files to the modern WebP format.

Privacy

We take privacy seriously. All image processing happens locally in your browser. We do not store, transmit, or have access to any images you process on this site.
