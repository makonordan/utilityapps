About This Calculator

This mortgage calculator uses the standard amortization formula to compute your monthly principal and interest (P&I) payment. Each month, a portion of your payment goes toward interest — calculated on your remaining balance — and the rest reduces your principal.

Extra payments are applied directly to your principal, which reduces the balance on which future interest is calculated. Even a small extra monthly payment can significantly cut total interest and payoff time.

PMI (Private Mortgage Insurance) is typically required when your down payment is less than 20% of the home's value. It usually ranges from 0.2% to 2% of the loan amount annually.
