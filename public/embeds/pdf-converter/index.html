Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF to Word converter really free?
Yes, completely free. There are no hidden fees, no premium tiers, and no credit card required. We keep the tool running with unobtrusive ads.
Does it work without creating an account?
Absolutely. You never need to sign up or provide an email address. Just upload your PDF and convert — no strings attached.
Is my PDF file secure and private?
Your file is sent over an encrypted HTTPS connection and is automatically deleted from our servers immediately after the conversion is complete. We never store, read, or share your documents.
What is the maximum file size?
The maximum supported file size is 10 MB. Most PDFs — including multi-page reports and contracts — fall well within this limit.
Will the formatting be preserved?
For text-based PDFs, formatting such as fonts, headings, bullet points, tables, and page layout is preserved as accurately as possible. Complex multi-column layouts may require minor manual adjustment after conversion.
Can I convert scanned PDFs?
Scanned PDFs (image-only) have limited support. The converter works best with PDFs that contain real embedded text. If your scanned document was processed with OCR before being saved, results will be much better.
How long does conversion take?
Most conversions complete in 5–15 seconds, depending on the file size and complexity. Large PDFs with many images may take up to 30 seconds.
What browsers are supported?
The converter works in all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge — on both desktop and mobile. No plugins or extensions are required.
