Overview

The short version: we do not collect personal information, we do not store your files, and we do not sell your data to anyone.

PDF2Word (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we handle when you use our free PDF conversion tools at pdf2word.io (the “Service”) and how we handle it.

By using the Service you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Service.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal information from users of this Service. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not ask for your name, email address, or any contact details.
  • We do not collect payment information — the Service is entirely free.
  • We do not build user profiles or track you across other websites.

Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, referring URL, date/time) may be retained for up to 30 days for security and abuse prevention, after which they are automatically purged. These logs are not linked to individual users or uploaded files.

File Handling

Your privacy regarding uploaded files is our highest priority:

  • Files are transmitted to our conversion API over an encrypted HTTPS connection.
  • Files are processed for the sole purpose of performing the requested conversion.
  • Files are deleted from the processing server immediately after the converted output is generated — typically within seconds.
  • We do not permanently store any uploaded file on our own servers.
  • We do not read, analyse, index, or share the contents of your documents.
  • Converted output files are served directly to your browser and are not stored after download.

Despite these protections, we recommend that you avoid uploading documents that contain highly sensitive personal data (e.g. passports, medical records, financial statements) to any online conversion service, including ours. For those use cases, consider using offline desktop software.

Cookies & Advertising

We use cookies in the following limited ways:

Functional cookies

We store a single counter in your browser’s localStorage to display how many files you have converted today. This never leaves your device and is not accessible to any third party.

Google AdSense cookies

This site displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google’s Ad Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of third-party cookies for interest-based advertising through aboutads.info. Google’s use of advertising cookies is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

We do not use any other tracking cookies, session cookies, or marketing pixels beyond those described above.

Analytics

We may add Google Analytics or a privacy-friendly alternative (such as Plausible or Fathom) in the future to understand aggregate usage patterns — for example, which tools are most popular and where visitors come from.

If and when analytics are added, this policy will be updated to describe exactly what is collected, how it is used, and how you can opt out. No analytics scripts are loaded at this time.

Third-Party Services

To provide the conversion service, your uploaded file is sent to a third-party API. We currently use:

ConvertAPI (Privacy Policy ↗)

Processes the actual file conversion. ConvertAPI deletes uploaded files from their servers within one hour of processing. Files are transmitted over TLS encryption.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your files or any information about you with any third party except as described above and as strictly necessary to provide the Service.

We may also disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal process (e.g. a court order or subpoena). Given that we retain no files and no personal data, there is typically nothing to disclose.

Your Rights (GDPR & General Privacy)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with applicable privacy legislation, you have the following rights with respect to any personal data we hold:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("the right to be forgotten").
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to restrict processing: Ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: Object to processing of your personal data for direct marketing or legitimate interests.

Because we collect no personal data beyond transient server logs (which contain only IP addresses and are purged within 30 days), exercising most of these rights will confirm that we hold nothing. To make a request, contact us at the address below.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe we have not handled your data correctly.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal data to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
