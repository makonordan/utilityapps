Overview

PercentCalc ("we", "our", or "us") operates percentcalc.com. This page explains what data we collect, what we do not collect, and how advertising on the site works. The short version: we do not collect any personal information, we do not require an account, and we do not share data with third parties beyond what Google AdSense requires to serve ads.

No Personal Data Collected

PercentCalc does not collect, store, or process any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-up is required to use any calculator.
  • No name, email address, or contact information is collected.
  • All calculator inputs (numbers, percentages, prices) are processed entirely in your browser. Nothing you type is sent to our servers.
  • We do not use analytics tools that track individual users or build profiles.

Google AdSense & Advertising Cookies

PercentCalc displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. This is standard behaviour for ad-supported websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings. You may also opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data from sites that use its advertising services, see Google's Privacy & Terms.

Cookies

PercentCalc does not set any first-party cookies. The only cookies present on the site are set by Google AdSense for advertising purposes, as described in the section above. If you browse with an ad-blocker or with cookies disabled, the calculators will work exactly the same — no functionality depends on cookies.

No Data Shared With Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. No user data is shared with any company, advertiser, or data broker. The only third-party involvement on this site is Google AdSense, whose data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

PercentCalc does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The site contains no registration forms and gathers no personal data, so no special handling is required. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our site, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date below. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
